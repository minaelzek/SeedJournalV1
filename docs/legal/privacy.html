SeedJournal Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-30. Draft — review with counsel before launch.

SeedJournal is a private reflection journal.

What we collect

Account data from Sign in with Apple (identifier and optional email). Journal entries you save. Derived memories and embeddings for search and tree growth — not sold or used for advertising.

How we use data

To operate the journal, optional AI reflection, memory search, and tree visualization. We do not use your journal for third-party model training unless you explicitly opt in.

Your choices

Export or delete your account in the app Settings.

Not medical care

SeedJournal is not therapy or crisis services.

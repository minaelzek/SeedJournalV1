# SeedJournal Privacy Policy (Draft)

**Last updated:** 2026-03-30

SeedJournal is a private reflection journal. This draft is for App Store submission—have counsel review before publishing.

## What we collect

- **Account:** Apple Sign-In identifier and optional email (including Apple private relay).  
- **Journal content:** Entries you choose to save.  
- **Derived data:** AI-extracted memories and embeddings used for search and tree growth signals—not sold or used for advertising.

## How we use data

- Operate the journal, optional AI reflection, memory search, and tree visualization.  
- We do **not** use your journal for third-party model training unless you explicitly opt in (default: off).

## Storage & security

- Data transmitted over TLS.  
- Stored on our servers with access controls.  
- Application logs do not include full journal bodies.

## Your choices

- Export data (`Settings → Export`).  
- Delete account (`Settings → Delete account`).  
- Disable AI depth (when exposed in settings API).

## Not medical care

SeedJournal is not therapy or crisis services. See in-app crisis resources for emergencies.

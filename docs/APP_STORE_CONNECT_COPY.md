## Description

SeedJournal is a premium private journal for young adults who want depth without noise.

Write freely. When you choose, a gentle AI guide asks one or two thoughtful questions—then you decide when the session ends. Your words become personal memories you can search later. A living sakura tree on the home screen reflects seasons of your reflection, not gamified points.

**What SeedJournal is**

- Private. Only you see your journal.
- Calm. No tab bar clutter, no social feed.
- Yours. Continue reflecting or save and close—every time.

**What SeedJournal is not**

- Not therapy, medical advice, or crisis support. If you are in crisis, use local emergency services or the resources in Settings.

Sign in with Apple. Export or delete your data anytime in Settings.
